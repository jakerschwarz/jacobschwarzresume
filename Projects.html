Senior Seminar Research Paper

This project involves a case study of the supreme court case, CYAP v. Wilson in 2023. This case study examines the effectiveness of removing the ability for juvenilles to be charged with adult crimes, such as Disorderly Conduct for normal juvenille behaviors in school. The case study compares South Carolina to other states, such as Texas that have no gotten rid of these laws which allow for juvenilles to be charged with adult crimes in school.
